Community Guidelines

Our Mission: Creating a safe, respectful environment where active singles can find meaningful connections through shared sports and activities.

Be Authentic

  • Use recent, accurate photos of yourself
  • Provide truthful information in your profile
  • Be genuine in your interactions
  • Don't catfish or misrepresent yourself

Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with kindness and respect
  • Accept rejection gracefully
  • Respect others' boundaries and preferences
  • No harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior

Be Safe

  • Never share personal information (address, phone, financial details) early
  • Meet in public places for first dates
  • Tell someone where you're going
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, it probably is
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately

Prohibited Content

The following content is not allowed on Peac:

  • Nudity or sexually explicit material
  • Hate speech or discriminatory language
  • Violence or threats
  • Spam or promotional content
  • Illegal activities or substances
  • Copyrighted material without permission

Activity-Focused Dating

  • Share your genuine sports and activity interests
  • Be open to trying new activities with matches
  • Suggest active dates that both parties enjoy
  • Respect different skill levels and abilities

Reporting and Enforcement

  • Report any violations of these guidelines
  • We review all reports promptly
  • Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent bans
  • We may remove content or profiles without warning

Privacy and Data

  • Don't share screenshots of conversations without consent
  • Respect others' privacy
  • Don't use the platform to collect data about users

Remember: Our community thrives when everyone feels safe, respected, and able to be themselves. Help us maintain a positive environment for all active singles!

Questions or Concerns?
